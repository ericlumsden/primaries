The graphs below are meant to accompany my blog post about building an interactive web graphic by investigating the correlation between a sitting president's approval rating and the number of individuals competing for the opposing party's nomination.
Click on the data points to get a readout of the year, sitting President, their approval ratings and a list of the individuals running for the opposing party's nomination.
Green dots indicate a sitting President whose party won the election; red indicates a loss for the sitting President's party.
Grey is for our current President.
